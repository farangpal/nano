    Black box
    What's Inside

    Mysterious ‘NANOPASSES’ have been smuggled into our world by Agents #0 and #1... Once a week, these passes are able to breach the network and plunder data fragments from within.By pure chance, an anonymous engineer cracked their code and developed 5,555 passes.These data fragments are called [BLACK BOXES]. They could contain a dazzling array of valuable items... but only if you're fortunate enough to lay your hands on one that is uncorrupted.

    The Positive Feedback Loop
    How does it work

    Post mint, multiple functions will synergize to create a positive feedback loop which works to keep holders invested and maintain a high level of demand for NANOPASSES.[BLACK BOXES] are the centrepiece to the loop, they will act as the place where value is continuously generated and compounded. Here's how it works.

    ROYALTIES

    75% of ongoing royalties from secondary markets will be funnelled into the Community Vault. This is what will be used to fund weekly [BLACK BOX] prizes for our passholders.

    BLACK BOXES

    Each week the community votes on how the funds in the vault be allocated. What prizes would be distributed that week? Perhaps commiting the week's funding to a charitable cause? It is all decided by you, the holders of the passes.

    PHASE 2+

    NANOPASS is just a prequel for what's to come in our ambitious Main Storyline. With the release of each phase in the future, the storyline will be driven forward. As passes guarantee access into the future parts of the project, this will become a part of the positive feedback loop that adds further value to holders.

    TRADING VOLUME

    Perpetual BLACK BOXES keep the community centered around an essential function each week, engaging our collective efforts and bringing people back to the project over and over to build a strong network of diamond handers - this creates scarcity. Phase 2+ will boost the demand side by increasing the desirability of NANOPASSES, forming a positive feedback loop of higher floors and higher volumes. Royalties from these sales will flow directly into the Community Vault...

    COSMETICS & ITEMS

    We'd want our earliest supports to look and feel special in the Nanoverse. Ultra rare loot tied to the future phases of the project will be added to [BLACK BOXES]. These gifts could be in the form of cosmetics, items, or even upgrades. Expect plenty of seasonal events and goodies that are made exclusively available only to pass holders in the future.
